Lionel Messi

Football Legend

Argentine Professional Footballer

Lionel Messi, in full Lionel Andrés Messi, also called Leo Messi, (born June 24, 1987, Rosario, Argentina), Argentine-born football (soccer) player who received a record-setting seven Ballon d’Or awards as the world’s top male player (2009–12, 2015, 2019, and 2021). In 2022 he helped Argentina win the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA)’s World Cup.

Career Highlights

  • 1995

    Began playing football for Newell's Old Boys youth team in Rosario, Argentina at age 8.

  • 1998

    At age 11, diagnosed with growth hormone deficiency. Barcelona offered to pay for his medical treatment if he moved to Spain.

  • 2000

    At age 13, relocated to Spain to join Barcelona's youth academy La Masia.

  • 2003

    Made his competitive debut for Barcelona at age 16 against Porto.

  • 2005

    At 17, made first team debut in La Liga. Became youngest player to score for Barcelona.

  • 2009

    Helped Barcelona win historic treble - La Liga, Copa del Rey, UEFA Champions League.

  • 2010

    Named FIFA Ballon d'Or winner for the first time.

  • 2011

    Scored most goals in a La Liga season (50) and became Champions League top scorer.

  • 2012

    Broke Barcelona's all-time goalscoring record. Scored 91 goals for club and country.

  • 2014

    Led Argentina to World Cup final. Won Golden Ball as the tournament's best player.

  • 2015

    Named La Liga's all-time leading goalscorer. Won record 5th FIFA Ballon d'Or.

  • 2019

    Won a record 6th Ballon d'Or and became first player to win three straight.

  • 2021

    Broke Pele's South American men's goalscoring record. Won Copa America with Argentina.

  • 2022

    Led Argentina to World Cup glory in Qatar. Scored 7 goals including in final vs France.

Greatest Goals

Incredible solo goal vs Athletic Bilbao in 2015 Copa Del Rey final
